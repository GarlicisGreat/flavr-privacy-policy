# Privacy Policy for Flavr

**Effective date:** 18 January 2026

Flavr (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Flavr mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use Flavr, we may collect the following information:

### Personal Information
- Email address (used for account creation and login)

### User-Generated Content
- Recipes  
- Favorites  
- Cookbooks  
- Shopping lists  
- Any other content you choose to create or save in the app  

### Technical Information
- Basic app usage and device information, such as app performance and crash data

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Create and manage your user account
- Save and sync your recipes and preferences across devices
- Provide core app functionality
- Improve app performance and reliability
- Communicate with you regarding support or account-related issues

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

Flavr uses trusted third-party services to operate the app, including:
- Firebase Authentication for secure login
- Firebase Firestore or Database for storing app data
- Firebase Storage for storing images and media
- Firebase Analytics (if enabled) to understand app usage and improve features

These services may collect information as governed by their own privacy policies.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

- We do not sell your personal data
- We do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes
- Data is only shared with service providers strictly necessary to operate the app (such as Firebase)

---

## 5. Data Retention

Your data is stored only for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide app functionality.  
You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Delete your account and all associated content

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the email address below.
